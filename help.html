Help & guidelines

How to use the Ocean View reservation system

1. User authentication

Use Login to sign in with your email and password. New guests can Register to create an account with name, email, phone, and password. Keep your password secure.

2. Browsing and searching rooms

Go to Rooms to see all available room types. You can filter by:

  • Room type (Standard, Deluxe, Ocean View, Luxury Suite)
  • Price range (min and max price per night)
  • Sea view (yes/no)

Click View & book on a room to see full details and amenities.

3. Making a reservation

On the room details page:

  • Select your check-in and check-out dates. Check-in must be today or later; check-out must be after check-in.
  • Click Check availability & book to proceed.
  • Enter guest details (name, email, phone, address) and confirm the booking.

You will receive a unique reservation number (e.g. OV-YYYYMMDD-XXX). Save this number for your records.

4. Viewing reservation details

After booking, the confirmation page shows your reservation number, room, dates, and total cost. From My Bookings you can view all past and upcoming reservations and open the full details for each.

5. Bill calculation

The total cost is calculated as: (number of nights) × (room price per night). The number of nights is based on your check-in and check-out dates. The confirmation page and booking history show the total amount.

6. Cancelling a booking (optional)

If the option is enabled, you can cancel a booking from My Bookings by selecting the reservation and choosing Cancel. Cancellation policy may apply; contact the hotel for details.

7. Exiting the system

Use Logout to end your session safely. Closing the browser may also end the session after a period of inactivity.
